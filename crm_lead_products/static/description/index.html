E-Chat Integration (Viber, Telegram, WhatsApp)

Bring your E-Chat conversations into Odoo 18 – inbound webhooks, outbound sending, operator auto-assignment, CRM & Discuss.

Highlights

  • Viber / Telegram / WhatsApp (number) support via E-Chat.
  • Route messages to CRM Leads or Discuss (per connection).
  • Operator assignment: reuse last agent or round-robin by workload.
  • Attachments in & out (each file sent as a separate request).
  • Fast setup with simple per-channel adapters.
